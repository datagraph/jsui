Who You Are

This section is about your account. Your account is a collection of information that says who you are. Or at least, who you claim to be. The computer believes you. It has to.

What You'll See

When you log in, you get to see things about yourself:

  • Your username (you picked this, remember?)
  • Your email address
  • Your full name
  • Your homepage and blog (if you have them)
  • Your company
  • Where you live
  • Your repositories (boxes full of data)

How to Change Things

Step 1: Go to your account page. Click your name somewhere.

Figure 1: Double-click. Type. Simple.

Step 2: Find the thing you want to change.
Step 3: Double-click on it. It turns into a text box.
Step 4: Type the new value.
Step 5: Press Enter or click somewhere else.
Step 6: Click the Save button. This is important.

Figure 2: The Save button. Click it.

The computer only sends what changed. It's efficient that way. It doesn't send everything every time. Just the bits that are different now.
Important: If you don't click Save, nothing happens. Well, nothing permanent. Your changes disappear when you leave. Click Save.

Your Token

You have an authentication token. It's like a secret password for computers. Programs use it to access your account without typing passwords.

Finding Your Token

  1. Go to your account page
  2. Look for the Token section
  3. There it is

Getting a New Token

Sometimes you need a new one:

  1. Find the token section
  2. Click Reset Token
  3. New token appears
  4. Update anything that was using the old one

Figure 3: The Reset button. Use it carefully.

Security: Your token is secret. Don't share it. Anyone with your token can access your account. If you think someone else has it, reset it immediately.

Getting Around

From your account page, you can:

  • See all your repositories
  • Create new repositories
  • Go to specific repositories
  • Do repository things

Multiple Accounts

You can have more than one account active. Maybe you have a work account and a personal account. Maybe you're testing something. Whatever the reason, it works:

  • Switch between accounts
  • Work with different repositories
  • Each account has its own session

Troubleshooting

Changes Won't Save

  • Did you click the Save button?
  • Check for error messages
  • Make sure you have permission
  • Check your internet

Token Gone

  • Are you on the right account?
  • Try refreshing
  • Check your permissions
